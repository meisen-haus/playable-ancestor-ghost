6. WHY EACH PIPELINE BEHAVES THE WAY IT DOES
========================================================================
STRETCHED HANDS (creature weights on Bip01, early exports):
  - Mesh WAS skinned; vertices followed bones at runtime.
  - Creature finger bones (Finger02/12/22...) don't exist on Bip01 -> spikes to origin.
  - Even after name remap, bind matrices differ (~0.55u on shared hand bones).
  - Result: wrong pose but SKELETON-CONNECTED.

CURRENT GRAFT (static mesh + copy vanilla NiSkinInstance + bbox-fit):
  - _apply_modifiers BAKES Bip01.001 armature -> destroys 11 weight groups.
  - Weights stripped; vanilla skin pasted with nearest-vertex on reshaped mesh.
  - Vert positions forced into vanilla bbox (+ 1.5x scale) -> looks placed but
    weight/bind relationship is wrong -> invisible, inside body, or rubbery.

APPLY-ARMATURE TRAP:
  _apply_modifiers() on creature hands collapses all 11 groups to 1 (Finger21).
  Never apply the creature armature modifier when preserving weights.
